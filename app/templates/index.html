Overview

We have created a AI product that predicts different economic statistics in a select basket of countries, while providing valuable insights into the interplay of different factors within their economies. It has wide-ranging potential, and while the predictive ability is not always accurate, due to the inherent unpredictability of the economy, it still shows us useful correlations.
