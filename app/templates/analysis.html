Overview

Prediction Model and Use

The model we have created is able to predict a handful of different facets of an economy via their relation to other recorded data. For example, the model could predict the GDP of a given country using the year or it could predict the GDP using gdp growth, lending rate, unemployment etc. However, it is important to first consider the the correlation to factors might have before trying to predict anything.

What is GDP?

GDP is simply the measured market value of all of the products and services a country provides/produces. GDP often correlates strongly with the year in countries that have a stable economic position such as the US. For this reason GDP is one of the more accurate predictions you can make using our model

Structured Through Python

Our AI is coded using the coding language Python. Python has a multitude of uses but it is particularly good at machine learning and data analysis, making it a perfect match for our project.

What is Linear Regression

Linear Regression is a form of supervised machine learning. It predicts the output of continuous data on a consistent slope. the graphs below both are products of our linear regression model.

US GDP over time



France's GDP over time



What is Bagging

Bootstrap aggregation, known as bagging, is an ensemble learning method. In the process of bagging approximately 80% of the data is randomly assigned to separate models, which means a single piece of data can be chosen more than once. These individual models are trained and tested separately and the average of all of these models is the final prediction.

Linear Regression vs Bagging

The main difference that sets Linear Regression apart from Bagging, is Linear Regression only uses a single model, which can lead to a larger area of incorrect and less accurate data prediction, while bagging uses a large amount of smaller models and the average of these smaller models tends to be more precise and reduce the ‘noise’ in the data.

What is XGBoost

XGBoost, which stands for Extreme Gradient Boosting, is a gradient boosted trees algorithm which is a supervised learning algorithm. XGBoost can also be used for both Regression and Classification problems. XgBoost works by creating and training many smaller and weaker models and taking the average of their outputs. XGBoost uses Boosting, Bagging, Gradient Boosting and other algorithm to make the most accurate prediction possible.
